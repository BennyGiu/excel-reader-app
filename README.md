# About

This is a simple single-page application (SPA) that, if you have the right files, can show you charts and stats of your electricity consumption

### Introduction

In order to use the application, you need to get the .csv files of your electricity consumption and they have to be in the right format, I got mine from the "E-Distribuzione" android application. You can check the format [here](https://github.com/BennyGiu/excel-reader-app/blob/master/public/2024-04.csv).

### Functionality

You can upload more than one month at once. After the files are uploaded you can choose of which month you want to see the chart or stats, when viewing the chart of a month, you can click on the bar of a day and view its chart, within the day view, you can choose to display the consumption per hour, per half hour or per quarter hour. If you want to test the application you can click on "Usa dati di prova" to use the app with three test months.
